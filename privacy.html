Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 22, 2026
Last Updated: January 22, 2026

ADMENSION ("we", "us", or "our") operates https://garebear99.github.io/ADMENSION/ (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our link shortening and ad monetization service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you visit our Site:

  • Browser Information: Browser type, version, language preference
  • Device Information: Device type (desktop/mobile), screen resolution, operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, navigation paths, button clicks
  • IP Address: For geographic location detection (country/region level only)
  • Session Data: Anonymous session identifiers stored locally in your browser

1.2 Information You Provide

When you create ADMENSION links, you may optionally provide:

  • Cryptocurrency Wallet Address: For receiving revenue share payouts
  • Link Metadata: Custom link names, destination URLs, custom messages

1.3 Local Storage

We use browser localStorage and sessionStorage to store:

  • Links you create (stored locally in your browser only)
  • Session statistics and activity tracking
  • User preferences and settings
  • Anti-fraud and abuse prevention data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

  • Service Operation: To provide link shortening and tracking services
  • Analytics: To understand traffic patterns and improve user experience
  • Revenue Distribution: To calculate and distribute advertising revenue fairly
  • Fraud Prevention: To detect and prevent abuse, invalid traffic, and ad fraud
  • Performance Optimization: To improve site speed and functionality
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may collect and use information about your visits to this and other websites to provide targeted advertising.

Google's Use of Information:

  • Google uses cookies and similar technologies to serve ads
  • Google may collect data about your browsing behavior across websites
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings
  • You can also opt out using the Digital Advertising Alliance's opt-out tool

Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3.2 Cloudflare Workers

We use Cloudflare Workers for API services and data storage. Cloudflare may process:

  • IP addresses for security and performance
  • Link creation and access data
  • Wallet addresses you optionally provide

Cloudflare's Privacy Policy: https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/

3.3 IP Geolocation Service

We use ipapi.co to detect your country/region for traffic analytics. This service processes IP addresses only.

ipapi.co Privacy Policy: https://ipapi.co/privacy/

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use the following tracking technologies:

4.1 Essential Cookies

  • Session Management: To maintain your session state
  • Anti-Fraud: To detect suspicious activity

4.2 Analytics Cookies

  • Usage Tracking: To understand how visitors use the Site
  • Performance Monitoring: To identify and fix technical issues

4.3 Advertising Cookies

  • Google AdSense: Third-party cookies for ad serving and targeting

Cookie Control: You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may limit Site functionality.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Where Data Is Stored

  • Your Browser: Links, preferences, and session data (localStorage/sessionStorage)
  • Cloudflare KV: Global link data and statistics (encrypted at rest)
  • Google Servers: Ad serving and analytics data

5.2 Data Retention

  • Link Data: 90 days from last activity (automatic expiration)
  • Session Data: Cleared when you close your browser
  • Local Storage: Persists until you clear browser data
  • Analytics: Aggregated data retained indefinitely for trend analysis

5.3 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • No collection of personally identifiable information (PII)
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Rate limiting and anti-fraud systems

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

6.1 Access and Deletion

  • Local Data: Clear your browser's localStorage to delete all local data
  • Global Links: Links expire automatically after 90 days of inactivity
  • Right to be Forgotten: Contact us to request deletion of specific link data

6.2 Opt-Out Rights

  • Personalized Ads: Use Google Ads Settings
  • Analytics: Use "Do Not Track" browser settings
  • All Tracking: Use private/incognito browsing mode

6.3 Data Portability

Your locally stored data can be exported using the "Export Logs" feature on the Stats page.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Site is not directed to individuals under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Our Site is hosted on GitHub Pages and Cloudflare's global network. Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. By using our Site, you consent to international data transfers.

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know: Request disclosure of personal information collected
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of the "sale" of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights

Note: We collect minimal personal information. Most data is anonymous and stored locally in your browser.

10. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of Access: Confirm whether we process your data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of data
  • Right to Restriction: Limit processing of your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Object to data processing
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

Legal Basis for Processing: We process data based on legitimate interests (service operation, fraud prevention) and consent (optional wallet addresses).
